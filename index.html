About Me

Greetings, I am Shivansh, currently working as a Senior Developer at UST Innovation Labs. My interests lie mainly in autonomous mobile robots, drones, and prosthesis development. I am also particulary passionate about interfacing AI and LLM's into robotics. I've participated in several national and international competitions and was the former head of The Robotics Club at IIT Palakkad.

I have done various projects in robotics, utilizing both ROS and ROS 2, and have integrated AI into various systems. I also have extensive experience interfacing robots with various simulation environments, including Gazebo-classic, Ignition, Isaac Sim, and Isaac Lab. Additionally, I have developed 3D models and robotic systems using Fusion 360 and SolidWorks, and have worked with a wide range of sensors for autonomous development. Have a look at some of my projects below!
